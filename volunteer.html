Why Join Portland Digital Corps

Get unstuck from endless job searches and build something that matters.

This isn't just free work

Portland Digital Corps is a direct response to two realities:

  1. Job searching is broken. Endlessly updating portfolios, tailoring resumes for ATS systems, and attending networking events isn't working for many talented people.
  2. Organizations need help. Local nonprofits and community groups have digital challenges that are going unsolved because they can't afford market rates.

Instead of spinning our wheels individually, we're creating a structured space to build real work for real organizations that need it. The focus is on short-term, high-impact projects that:

  • Give you concrete portfolio pieces that demonstrate leadership and teamwork
  • Connect you directly with Portland's tech and civic communities
  • Provide a structured opportunity to practice your craft
  • Actually ship something useful for organizations doing important work

This is not:

  • An ongoing free labor pipeline or permanent organization
  • A replacement for paid consulting work
  • A guarantee of future employment

It's a focused sprint with defined boundaries to help break the isolation of job searching while making meaningful contributions.

Who We're Looking For

Skills We Need

  • Design: UX, UI, service design, content design
  • Development: Front-end, back-end, full-stack
  • Product: Product management, project coordination
  • Research: User research, testing, data analysis

Time Commitment

We've designed our projects to work with different schedules:

  • Core volunteers: 5-10 hours per week
  • Project duration: 4-8 weeks total
  • Team meetings: Weekly, remote or hybrid

You'll never be asked to sacrifice job search time or interviews for these projects.

What You'll Get

Leadership Experience

Take on roles that demonstrate your ability to lead, collaborate, and ship work with real constraints.

Portfolio Material

Add concrete case studies to your portfolio that show real problem-solving, not just spec work.

Local Connections

Build meaningful relationships with other tech professionals and organizations in Portland.
