Volunteer with Portland Digital Corps

Use your tech skills to help local organizations build better digital tools and services.

Who We're Looking For

Skills We Need

  • Design: UX, UI, service design, content design
  • Development: Front-end, back-end, full-stack
  • Product: Product management, project coordination
  • Research: User research, testing, data analysis
  • Technical: DevOps, security, accessibility

Time Commitment

We've designed our projects to be flexible:

  • Core volunteers: 5-10 hours per week
  • Project duration: Typically 4-8 weeks
  • Team meetings: Weekly, remote or hybrid

We'll match you to projects based on your skills and availability.

What You'll Get

Real Impact

Work on projects with concrete outcomes that help Portland-area organizations and the communities they serve.

Portfolio Building

Gain experience and case studies for your portfolio, especially valuable if you're between jobs or early in your career.

Network Growth

Connect with other civic-minded tech professionals and organizations in the Portland area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to live in Portland?

While most of our work is remote, we prioritize volunteers in the Portland metro area who can occasionally meet in person. However, we welcome applications from anywhere in Oregon or SW Washington.

What if I can only volunteer a few hours?

We can always use specialized help for targeted tasks. Let us know your constraints in the application, and we'll find a way to involve you that matches your availability.

How experienced do I need to be?

We welcome all skill levels! Junior folks often pair with more experienced volunteers. What matters most is your willingness to learn and contribute.

When will projects start?

Our first cohort of projects will kick off in May 2025, with new projects starting on a rolling basis after that.
