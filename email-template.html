Welcome to PDX Digital Corps!

Thanks so much for signing up to volunteer with PDX Digital Corps! We’re thrilled to have you on board. Whether you’re a designer, technologist, researcher, or just someone who wants to support local nonprofits, we’re building this together — and there’s a place for you.

Step 1: Join us on Discord
This is where we coordinate, share updates, and post volunteer opportunities.

Join the Discord

Once you're in, say hi in #introductions and we’ll get you sorted.

If you're just looking to follow along, our newsletter goes out regularly. You can sign up here or subscribe to our RSS feed.

We’re also on GitHub — you can check out active projects, issues, and documentation there.

PDX Digital Corps is a short-term, all-volunteer experiment in community-scale tech support. We’re proving what’s possible when people show up and ship meaningful work together.

Thanks again for signing up. You’re what makes this work possible.
