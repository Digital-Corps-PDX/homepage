Our Approach to Volunteer Projects

Portland Digital Corps is a volunteer initiative designed to address specific digital challenges through focused, time-limited projects. We believe in:

  • Clear value exchange — Volunteers provide their skills. Organizations provide a meaningful problem to solve.
  • Defined boundaries — We scope projects that can realistically be completed in 4-8 weeks.
  • Mutual respect — This is a specific engagement to address specific challenges.

How We Can Help

Types of Projects

  • Website improvements - fixing broken elements, improving accessibility
  • Form optimization - streamlining application or signup processes
  • Simple tech implementations - connecting systems, automating repetitive tasks
  • User research - understanding how people use your services

What Makes a Good Project

  • Clear scope - specific challenges we can address in 4-8 weeks
  • Available point person - someone from your org who can answer questions
  • Meaningful impact - something that truly helps your mission
  • Long-term value - solution you can maintain after we're done

What We Need From You

Your Commitments

  • Timely communication - responding to questions within 1-2 business days
  • Decision-making authority - access to someone who can make project decisions
  • Content and assets - providing text, images, and access needed for the project
  • Respect for scope - understanding the limitations of volunteer projects

Our Limitations

  • No ongoing maintenance - we help build it, but can't run it for you
  • No marketing or fundraising - we focus on digital tools, not campaigns
  • No full website redesigns - we target specific improvements
  • No large-scale development - we focus on targeted solutions
