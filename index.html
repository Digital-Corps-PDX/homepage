How It Works

1. Identify the need

We scope bite-sized projects with clear deliverables that can ship in weeks, not months.

2. Build expert teams

Designers, developers, and PMs form cross-functional teams matched to project needs.

3. Ship real solutions

No endless meetings. No vague promises. Just focused work that delivers results.

For Organizations

We help nonprofits, government entities, and community groups with digital projects that have been stuck on the back burner.

What we can help with:

  • Fixing broken websites and forms
  • Making digital tools more usable
  • Creating simple tools for specific needs
  • Digital accessibility improvements
Submit Your Project

What we don't do:

  • Ongoing maintenance or hosting
  • Marketing, branding, or fundraising
  • Full website redesigns or rebuilds
  • Enterprise-scale software development

Not sure if your project is a good fit? Come to our info session or submit your idea anyway – we're happy to help you figure it out.

Why This Matters

For Tech Professionals

Job searching is broken. Endlessly revising portfolios and chasing interviews is exhausting. This is a chance to:

  • Work on structured projects with clear deliverables
  • Break out of isolation and build local connections
  • Add meaningful work to your portfolio

For Organizations

Digital infrastructure is increasingly critical, but many orgs lack the resources to address it. This is a chance to:

  • Tackle specific digital challenges that are stuck
  • Work with skilled professionals who understand technology
  • Improve the digital experience for the communities you serve
