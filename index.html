Volunteer For Nonprofits FAQ

Helping Portland nonprofits go digital—fast.

Portland Digital Corps is an 8-week civic tech sprint that matches volunteer technologists with local orgs who need digital help. Websites, forms, simple tools—done right.

Join the Corps

How It Works

1. Nonprofits submit

We scope small digital projects—like fixing forms, updating sites, or building simple tools.

2. Volunteers form teams

We match technologists (devs, designers, PMs) to projects based on skill and availability.

3. 8-week sprints

Teams build, test, and deliver. We ship usable solutions fast—and everyone learns along the way.

Want to volunteer?

We're looking for designers, developers, and PMs who want to build public digital infrastructure the Portland way.
