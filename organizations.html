For Organizations

Get help with your digital challenges from skilled volunteers committed to Portland's community.

How We Can Help

Types of Projects

  • Website improvements - fixing broken elements, improving accessibility, updating content management
  • Form optimization - streamlining application, donation, or signup processes
  • Simple tech implementations - connecting systems, automating repetitive tasks
  • User research & testing - understanding how people use your services
  • Accessibility audits - identifying and fixing barriers to digital access

What Makes a Good Project

  • Clear scope - specific challenges we can address in 4-8 weeks
  • Available point person - someone from your org who can answer questions
  • Meaningful impact - something that truly helps your mission
  • Long-term value - solution you can maintain after we're done

Not sure if your project is a good fit? Submit it anyway, and we'll help you refine the scope or suggest alternatives.

Who Can Apply

Portland Digital Corps works with:

  • Nonprofit organizations (501c3, 501c4)
  • Local government entities and agencies
  • Community-based organizations and mutual aid groups
